## Emergency Playbook

### If HN Post Dies Quickly:
- Don't repost (against rules). Wait 2 weeks and try a different angle.
- Focus energy on Reddit r/ClaudeAI and Product Hunt instead.
- The Claude AI community is more likely to appreciate this than general HN.

### If Someone Asks "Why Not Just Use CLAUDE.md?":
- Acknowledge CLAUDE.md is great for single-project context
- Explain BizBrain builds a knowledge graph ACROSS all your projects, clients, entities
- The scanner discovers things you forgot to document
- Context compounds automatically — you don't have to manually maintain it
- Entity watchdog tracks people/companies across every conversation

### If Someone Clones/Forks Immediately:
- Don't panic. They can't use your name (trademark pending).
- Respond positively: "Forks are the highest form of flattery in open source."
- Your community, brand, and velocity are your moat.

### If a Bug Goes Viral:
- Fix it within hours.
- Post a transparent update on all channels.
- "We found a bug in X, here's the fix, and here's what we're doing to prevent it."

### If You Get Media Attention:
- Respond to journalist DMs within 1 hour.
- Have a press kit ready (logo, screenshots, one-pager).
- Key narrative: "The compound interest of AI context" — this is the soundbite.
